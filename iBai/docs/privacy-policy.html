iBai Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Overview

iBai is a multimedia client that connects to your personal media servers. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

iBai does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All data remains on your device or is communicated directly between your device and the media servers you configure (such as Emby, Plex, Navidrome, Audiobookshelf, WebDAV, SMB, Google Drive, or Dropbox).

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device using UserDefaults and local storage:

  • Server connection details (URLs, usernames, tokens) that you provide
  • Playback preferences and queue state
  • Cached media metadata for offline access
  • A device identifier (based on iOS identifierForVendor) used solely for media server authentication

Third-Party Services

iBai connects to the following third-party services only when you explicitly configure them:

  • Last.fm / ListenBrainz: If you enable scrobbling, your listening history is sent to these services per their privacy policies.
  • Google Drive / Dropbox: If you connect cloud storage, OAuth tokens are stored locally to access your files.

Analytics and Tracking

iBai does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage in any way.

Network Access

The app requires network access to communicate with your configured media servers. It also requires local network access to discover and connect to NAS devices on your local network.

Children's Privacy

iBai is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
